Introduction

The European Digital Identity Wallet (EUDIW) initiative represents a transformative approach to digital identity management, aimed at providing citizens with a secure, user-centric, and interoperable digital identity solution across the European Union.

As a member of the EWC concortium, DIGG is committed to:

  • Developing and testing the reference wallet implementation
  • Contributing to the standardization of digital identity wallets
  • Creating a Swedish-specific implementation of the EUDIW
  • Give partners in EWC the possibility to test issuing a swedish PID to wallets

We closely follow the latest specifications outlined in the EUDIW Architecture and Reference Framework.

Wallet Compatibility Disclaimer

We are actively working to support multiple wallets. Currently, we provide compatibility with:

  • Some wallets compliant with RFC003 from the EUDI Wallet RFCs
  • wwWallet

Our goal is to continuously expand and update our wallet support to ensure the broadest possible compatibility.
